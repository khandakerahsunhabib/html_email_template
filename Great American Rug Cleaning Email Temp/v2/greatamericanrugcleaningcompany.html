Here’s a look behind the scenes at how we
clean your area rugs.

Inspection

We carefully examine your rug to choose the proper cleaning steps for your unique rug.

Dusting

We gently remove any loose dirt, dust, lint... with combing and/ or tumbling your rugs in our dusting area.

Washing

We have the ability to use full water submersion to get the deep down dirt,
dust and pet dander from your rugs.

Spraying & Scrubbing

We continue to scrub and rinse away dirt that is trapped deep
within your rug’s fibers.

Rinsing

Once we’re satisfied your rug is thoroughly clean, we put it through one final rinse.

Combing & Drying

We carefully comb your rug to reset the nap (direction of the fibers
and allow your rugs to air dry.
